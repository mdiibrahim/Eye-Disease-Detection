🔬 What This App Does

This AI-powered system analyzes retinal images and detects the following eye conditions using a deep learning model:

  • 👁 Cataract – Clouding of the eye lens, blurs vision
  • 🩸 Diabetic Retinopathy – Damage to retina from high blood sugar
  • 🌫 Glaucoma – Pressure-induced optic nerve damage
  • ✅ Normal – No signs of disease, healthy retina
  • ❓ Unknown – The model couldn’t confidently match this image to any known disease class
